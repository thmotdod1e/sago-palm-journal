# Case Study: Chlorosis on Sago Palm #3

## Plant Details
- **ID:** Sago Palm #3
- **Location:** East patio (morning sun, afternoon shade)
- **Potting:** Container with well-draining mix
- **Age:** Approximately 5 years

## Initial Observation (Week 1)
**Date:** December 27, 2025

**Symptoms:**
- Newest fronds showing interveinal chlorosis (yellowing between veins)
- Veins remain dark green
- No signs of pest infestation (no scale, mites, or webbing)
- Older fronds appear healthy and green
- Soil surface appears moist

**Hypothesis:**
Potential manganese deficiency, common in cycads grown in alkaline soils or with poor nutrient availability.
